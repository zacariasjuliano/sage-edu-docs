# User Manual - SAGE Education

SAGE Education is an integrated Information Technology system applied to education, developed to support the academic and administrative management of educational institutions.
With a modular, secure, and scalable architecture, the platform allows for the management of entities, enrollments, classes, assessments, academic trajectories, and other processes essential to school operation.

This manual presents the functionalities, operations, and procedures necessary for the efficient use of the modules that make up the Academic Management System (AMS) and School Management System (SMS), serving as a reference guide for secretaries, teachers, management, and other users.
